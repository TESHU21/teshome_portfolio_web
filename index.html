What is website ?

A website is a collection of web pages and related content that is identified by a common domain name and published on at least one web server. Websites are typically dedicated to a particular topic or purpose, such as news, education, commerce, entertainment or social networking.

Introduction to Cloud Computing

At its core, cloud computing refers to the delivery of computing services over the internet. Instead of relying on physical hardware or local servers, cloud computing utilizes a network of remote servers hosted on the internet to store, manage, and process data.

What is Computer Networking?

Computer networking refers to the practice of connecting multiple computers and other devices together to facilitate communication and the sharing of resources. It involves the design, implementation, management, and maintenance of both hardware and software components that enable these connections.

What is digtal marketing ?

Digital marketing is the use of digital technologies and platforms to promote products, services, or brands. It includes strategies like SEO, social media marketing, content marketing, email marketing, and online advertising to reach and engage with a target audience online. It allows businesses to expand their reach, track performance, and make data-driven decisions.
